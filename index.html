Common questions

A few things people often ask about Hajj Guide.

Is Hajj Guide free?

Yes. Hajj Guide is completely free. There are no ads, no paid tiers, no sign-up. It is a planning aid built and maintained as a sadaqah jariyah.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once you have loaded the site at least once on your phone, every section works offline — useful in tents at Mina, in crowds at the Haram, or anywhere with poor signal.

Does it cover the four Sunni madhabs?

Yes. The Rulings section presents the position of each madhab — Hanafi, Shafi'i, Maliki and Hanbali — side by side, so you can confirm what your school holds. Always confirm with a qualified scholar of your madhab before acting on any ruling.

Where is my data stored?

All your data — flights, hotels, journal entries, custom stops — is stored only in your browser on your own device. Nothing is sent anywhere. Hajj Guide does not require a sign-up and does not have a server-side database for user content.

Can I print my itinerary and emergency card?

Yes. The Itinerary tab includes a Print full itinerary button that produces a single-document printout of every day of your trip, including any custom stops you have added. The Overview tab includes a Print emergency card button that produces a single A4 sheet with your operator details, hotels, Saudi emergency numbers, and a QR code for the 24-hour operator line.

Is this a fatwa or religious ruling?

No. Hajj Guide is a planning aid, not a fatwa. It summarises commonly-held positions for educational purposes only. Always confirm specific rulings with a qualified scholar of your madhab before acting on them.
